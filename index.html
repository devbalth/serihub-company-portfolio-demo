Our Background

SeriHub was born from the Startup673 National Hackathon Cycle 2, where our team tackled a critical problem in Brunei's agricultural sector: the lack of a centralized, efficient, and transparent supply chain connecting farmers and retailers. Winning the National Hackathon hosted by StartUp673 validated our idea, and with support from industry partners, SeriHub took the first step toward building a sustainable agricultural ecosystem for Brunei. We secured seed funding from LiveWIRE and gained the opportunity to develop SeriHub into a fully operational business.

Who We Are

SeriHub is a Brunei-based tech enabler on a mission to uplift local enterprises through intuitive digital platforms and targeted marketing services. We bridge businesses with their ideal audiences—whether it's a neighborhood café, a home-grown retailer, or an artisan producer—helping them thrive in today's connected marketplace.
